Online Classroom Using VR is a new way of learning that brings the classroom to life using Virtual Reality. Instead of traditional online lectures, students can now enter a virtual room where the professor teaches in a fully immersive environment. This allows for better interaction, real-time engagement, and a more realistic learning experience—just like being in a real classroom. It’s education reimagined for the digital age.
